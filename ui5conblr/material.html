UI5con 2017 was all about the great Community around UI5 and their mind-blowing ideas paving the path of the framework’s future. With UI5 Evolution and the new SAPUI5 Demo Kit, the UI5 Development teams announced to target modularization, increase performance and leverage emerging Technologies. Community projects such as the custom control library UI5Lab and custom built features playing around with modern trends like mixed and augmented reality complemented the conference agenda. As a final Highlight, a toast was given with the particularly for UI5con@SAP brewed delicious "PullToRefresh IPA" which wowed the crowd!

This so far biggest UI5con ever thrived on the fun, open-minded atmosphere typical for UI5cons: enthusiastic people, rich discussions and entertaining "Anki Overdrive" races in the breaks. Originally initiated by the UI5 Community in March 2016, UI5con is meant to give UI5 developers and enthusiasts a common platform to meet face-to-face and discuss the latest innovations.

Now we’re looking forward to a breathtaking UI5con 2018, with loads of Community driven sessions – Call for Papers starting soon!
