WHAT & WHERE?
UI5con@SAP is a community driven conference all about the UI5 framework and topics related to it. It will be a full day event, packed with various lectures, hands-on sessions and demo booths in St. Leon-Rot, Germany
The official evening event takes place at Kalipeh Guesthouse, Walldorf. Opening at 7.30pm, we’d be happy to see you join us in celebrating the UI5 Community spirit!
From 8pm till 11pm there’ll be a rich buffet offering with vegetarian options as well as soft drinks, beer and wine for only 30€ per person. Don’t miss out on this fun evening among UI5 friends.
See below in Travel information for detailed info how to get there!
TRAVEL INFORMATION
UI5con@SAP takes place at the SAP ROT03 building, SAP-Allee 27, 68789 St. Leon-Rot. See this PDF for detailed information and how to get there.
Please note: the evening event is located in Kalipeh Guesthouse, Dietmar-Hopp-Allee 15, 69190 Walldorf, Germany. It’s a 10min ride from St. Leon-Rot to Walldorf, check this PDF for detailed information.
BY CAR
From Stuttgart or Hockenheim via Autobahn 6, take the Wiesloch/Rauenberg exit toward Walldorf. From Frankfurt or Basel via Autobahn 5, take the Walldorf/Wiesloch exit towards Walldorf. Get detailed information in this PDF. Numerous parking places are available at the event location where the guests of the conference can park.
BY BUS OR TRAIN
From Mannheim, Heidelberg or Karlsruhe take railway line S3 or S4 to the Rot-Malsch train station.
From there it is 15 minutes walk to the building 3. To reach it, take the west exit from the train station, follow the main road westwards, turn left at the first corner "Am Bahnhof". Follow the road until the end. After the parking lot take the foot way to the SAP building ROT03.
Alternatively, take bus 719 toward “Industriegebiet St. Leon-Rot, SAP” to bus stop “SAP-Allee”, turn right and follow the street until you see ROT03 up front.
BY PLANE
Most major air carriers service Frankfurt, Germany via Frankfurt Airport (FRA). You can easily reach Mannheim’s main train station from the Frankfurt Airport train station within 30min. Take the railway line S3 as described above to get to St. Leon-Rot.
HOTEL RECOMMENDATION
As the conference is located in St. Leon-Rot and the evening event takes place in Walldorf, we’d recommend to stay in one of the following hotels near those locations:
